# 01 — Probability & Statistics

## Syllabus Coverage
- Counting (permutations, combinations)
- Probability axioms, sample space, events
- Independent & mutually exclusive events
- Marginal, conditional, joint probability
- Bayes' Theorem
- Conditional expectation & variance
- Mean, median, mode, standard deviation
- Correlation & covariance
- Random variables (discrete & continuous)
- PMF, PDF, CDF
- Uniform, Bernoulli, Binomial distributions
- Exponential, Poisson, Normal, Standard Normal
- t-distribution, Chi-squared distribution
- Conditional PDF
- Central Limit Theorem
- Confidence intervals
- z-test, t-test, Chi-squared test

- "First Course in Probability" — Sheldon Ross
- YouTube: StatQuest, 3Blue1Brown
