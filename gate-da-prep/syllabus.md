## 1. Probability & Statistics

> `📅 Jun 13 – Jul 12` · `30 days` · `🟡 In progress`
> `Weightage ~16-18%` · `~23 sessions`
> `Importance ██████████` · `Difficulty ███████░░░` · `Effort ████████░░`

### 1.1 Counting Foundations
- Permutations (nPr) and combinations (nCr)
- Sampling with/without replacement
- Arrangements with repetitions, circular permutations
- Binomial theorem, multinomial coefficients

### 1.2 Probability Axioms & Fundamentals
- Sample space, events, set operations
- Axioms of probability (Kolmogorov)
- Independent events, mutually exclusive events
- Addition rule, multiplication rule
- Marginal, conditional, joint probability
- Law of total probability
- Bayes' Theorem

### 1.3 Random Variables
- Discrete vs continuous random variables
- Probability Mass Function (PMF), Probability Density Function (PDF)
- Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF)
- Conditional PDF
- Expectation, variance, moments
- Conditional expectation & variance
- Law of Iterated Expectation
- Covariance, correlation

### 1.4 Standard Distributions
- **Discrete:** Uniform, Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson
- **Continuous:** Uniform, Exponential, Normal, Standard Normal
- t-distribution, Chi-squared distribution
- Relations between distributions (Poisson→Exponential, Binomial→Normal etc.)

### 1.5 Descriptive Statistics
- Mean, median, mode, standard deviation
- Quantiles, percentiles, IQR
- Skewness, kurtosis

### 1.6 Estimation & Inference
- Central Limit Theorem
- Confidence intervals
- z-test, t-test, Chi-squared test
